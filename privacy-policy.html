Privacy Policy — VIA Route Inspector

Last updated: April 2026

VIA Route Inspector is a route compliance tool for the VIA Chapter III ultra-cycling race. This policy explains what data the app uses and how.

Data We Collect

None. VIA Route Inspector does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

GPX Files

GPX route files you import are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded to any server.

Location

The app may request access to your device location to display it on the map. Your location is never recorded, transmitted, or stored.

Third-Party Services

  • Open-Meteo — weather forecasts for race gates are fetched from open-meteo.com. No personal data is sent.
  • Google Maps — tapping "Open in Google Maps" on a POI opens the Google Maps app, subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — live POI data (water, campsites, toilets) is fetched from the Overpass API. No personal data is sent.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/jvail1/VIA_ROUTE_INSPECTOR.
